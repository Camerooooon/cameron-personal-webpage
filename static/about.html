Photography

I love taking photographs, you can find my favorite photographs in my portfolio. Most of the photographs I have taken were from the Sacramento area however I often bring my camera with me when I hike and go camping. My main camera is a Sony a6400 and my most commonly used lens is a 18-150mm kit lens. My favorite subjects are mountains and trees but I take all kinds of photos ranging from landscapes to buzzing bees. If you are interested in purchasing a print of any of my art work or you are interested in photography please email me.

Programming

I've been programming since 2013, seriously, I've found dated notebooks where I've hand written working python code into the pages. It's no secret that computers have been a big part of my life, to this day I still focus on learning anything I can about computers. Currently, my absolute favorite programming language is Rust. The type safety and costless abstraction presented by the Rust programming language is why I love it so much. My first programming language was Python, which is my personal recommendation for new programmers choosing an new language. Currently I'm a part of the programming subteam on FRC team, Citrus Circuits. I also work on some personal projects that you can find on my GitHub. Finally, I maintain my server network where I self host a lot of the service I use in my daily life.

Amateur Radio

I'm an amateur radio operator, my call sign is KN6YZZ, usually you can hear my calling on the K6MVR repeater located on top of Mt.Vaca in the Sacramento area. I am a member of the Yolo Amateur Radio Society Radio Club and I regularly partake in club meetings and the weekly nets. I've been interested in ham radio since I got my license in 03/21/2023. Usually I bring my large antenna with me on hikes and connected it to my handheld in order to make simplex contacts. Currently I am working on upgrading my home station to a larger antenna system with an HF radio.

Hiking and Exploring

I love the outdoors and going outside. I try to go on a hike every week as long as I am not busy. My favorite hike in the Sacramento area is Stebbins Cold Canyon.
